Circuit Schematic & Parts List

Circuit Schematic




In this circuit, I have used 3 push buttons which are connected to power(5V) and ground with a 10k Ohm pull down resistor and is also connected to the arduino via a signal pin. The pull down resistors are responsible for preventing a short circuit whenever the button is not pressed, this allows the arduino to read a low signal without a short circuit. The button on the left is connected to pin 2. The button in the middle is connected to pin 3. The button on the right is connected to pin 4. The LCD 16x2 is connected with 4 wires, GND, VCC, SDA and SCL. The GND pin is connected to the ground on the breadboard, the VCC pin is connected to power(5V) on the breadboard and both the SDA, data pin, and SCL, clock pin, are connected directly to the arduinos SDA and SCL pins.
