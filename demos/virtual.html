Virtual Demonstration

Instructions

  1. Start the simulation
  2. The LCD will power on and will display instructions
  3. Press the rightmost button on the breadboard to start the game
  4. A question will then appear on the LCD
  5. Press the leftmost button for true or the middle button for false
  6. The LCD will then tell you if you got it right or wrong
  7. There are only 5 questions and they are fixed
  8. At the end of the game, your score will be presented
  9. To play again, press the reset button or rerun the simulation
